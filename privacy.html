Overview

Loom (the "Service") is operated by Borborizo Development ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Service.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use the Service.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: email address and authentication identifiers when you create an account or sign in.
  • Profile information: username, bio, avatar/banner URLs, and optional social links.
  • Content you upload: asset metadata (name, description, version, tags), images, media, and files you submit to the Service.
  • Social and community actions: friend requests, friend lists, blocks, and related messages or status fields you submit through the UI.
  • Support requests: the subject and message you send when you ask us for help or report an issue.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data: interactions with the Service, and basic device/browser information that may be collected by our hosting, CDN, and analytics providers.
  • Log data: IP address, timestamps, and request information that may appear in server logs or provider logs.

Information from third parties

The Service relies on third-party services and content delivery networks, which may receive information from your browser as part of providing their services. Examples may include:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, database, and storage services.
  • CDNs: Tailwind CDN, Cloudflare (Font Awesome), and Google Fonts.
  • Avatar generation: Dicebear (used for identicon-style avatars when you do not set an avatar).

How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service (accounts, profiles, uploads, discovery, and social features).
  • Moderate and review content to keep the registry safe (including reviewing uploads and enforcing rules).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Improve performance and user experience, including debugging and analytics.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security notices, and important account-related messages.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.

How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: with providers we use to operate the Service (for example, Firebase/Google and CDNs).
  • Legal and safety: to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, safety, and security of the Service and users.
  • Public content: information you choose to make public (for example, your username/profile, and asset listings you publish) may be visible to other users or visitors.
  • Support and moderation: your support requests and related account/content details may be reviewed by authorized moderators/curators to respond and enforce our rules.
  • With your consent: when you explicitly agree to share information.

Cookies And Local Storage

We and our providers may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and operate security features. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work if cookies are disabled.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You can request deletion of your account data as described below.

Your Rights And Choices

  • Access and update: you can update certain profile fields from within the Service.
  • Deletion: you can request account deletion by submitting a ticket through our Support page.
  • Opt-out: if we offer marketing emails in the future, you will be able to opt out via an unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (for example, under GDPR/UK GDPR or state privacy laws). If applicable, contact us and we will respond consistent with applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided personal information, submit a ticket through our Support page.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

  • Borborizo Development
  • Support: support ticket form (sign-in required)
